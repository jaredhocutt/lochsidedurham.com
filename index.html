Lochside Durham

Lochside Durham is a mixed townhome and single family community located off of Hwy 55 in Durham, NC, with a pool and clubhouse. Nestled minutes away from Southpoint Mall, Research Triangle Park, RDU International Airport, and Downtown Durham, it's the perfect place to live for both work and play.
